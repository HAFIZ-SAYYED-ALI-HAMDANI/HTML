PIAIC offers the following courses:

  • Artificail Intelligence
  • Cloud Native Computing
  • Blockchain Computing
  • Internet of things

Artificial Intelligence

The artificial intelligence program of the PIAIC takes the student through the foundation of the AI technology, via teaching programing in open source languages like python, Numpy,Pandas,Tensorflow 2.0 and Flask, and teaching deploying practical AI solutions and use cases and ending with real-life examples of modern day AI in practice.









Cloud Native Computing

The cloud Native computing program prepares the students to program in open source technologies like linux,Docker,Kubernetes,JavaScript,Node.Js,React and React Native while introducing students to development of microservices and software development technques on cloud platforms like AWS.









Blockchain computing

The main emphasis of Blockchain program is to understand the blockchains like Ethereum and Hyperledger (Fabric) and learn to program in languages such as Solidity,Javascript on framworks like Node.js.The program also introduces them to development of distributed apps(DAPPs),framworks like web3 and truffle and iie_depth understanding of Blockchain-as-a-service(Baas) and how to develop and offer an Initial Coin offering (ICO) of a crypto-token.









Internet of Things

The internet of Things program introduces the students to open source development technologies like Rust,Enbedded Rust,webassembly,hyper,Diesel,Google assistant and kuberneties while making them efficent in developing Microservices for IOT edge,IOT cloud ,and voice computing for device control.
